# Real-Time Market Dashboard: Track Stocks, Crypto & Forex in One Place

If you're checking Robinhood for stocks, CoinGecko for crypto, and XE for exchange rates, you're wasting time. A unified market dashboard puts everything in one view — so you see the full picture instantly.

Here's why real-time dashboards matter and how to set one up in under a minute.

## The Problem with Fragmented Market Data

Most investors and traders juggle multiple apps:

- **Stocks:** Robinhood, Yahoo Finance, or a brokerage app
- **Crypto:** CoinGecko, CoinMarketCap, or exchange apps
- **Forex:** XE, OANDA, or Google searches
- **News:** Bloomberg, CNBC, Twitter/X

That's 4-6 apps just to understand what's happening in markets. Each has different update speeds, interfaces, and alert systems. You miss cross-asset correlations because the data lives in silos.

## What a Real-Time Market Dashboard Does

A unified dashboard aggregates all market data into one screen:

- **Stock quotes** — Real-time prices for any US equity
- **Crypto prices** — Bitcoin, Ethereum, and thousands of altcoins
- **Forex rates** — Major and exotic currency pairs
- **Market indices** — S&P 500, NASDAQ, Dow Jones
- **Custom watchlists** — Your picks, your layout
- **Price alerts** — Cross-asset notifications
- **News feed** — Financial headlines in context

The key advantage: you see correlations. When the dollar strengthens, you immediately see how it affects your crypto and international stock holdings — all on one screen.
