# Best Free Stock Screeners in 2026: Find Winning Trades Faster

Finding the right stocks doesn't have to cost money. A good stock screener lets you filter thousands of equities by price, volume, market cap, sector, and technical indicators — helping you zero in on opportunities that match your strategy.

## What Is a Stock Screener?

A stock screener is a tool that filters stocks based on criteria you define. Want to find small-cap tech stocks with a P/E ratio under 15 and rising volume? A screener does that in seconds.

The best screeners combine real-time data, flexible filters, and clean interfaces — without requiring a paid subscription.

## Stock Screening Strategies That Work

### Value Investing Screen
- P/E ratio < 15
- Price-to-book < 1.5
- Dividend yield > 2%
- Market cap > $1B

### Momentum Screen
- 50-day SMA above 200-day SMA
- Relative volume > 2x average
- Price up > 5% in last month
- Average volume > 500K

### Growth Screen
- Revenue growth > 20% YoY
- EPS growth > 15% YoY
- Market cap $500M - $10B
- Sector: Technology or Healthcare
